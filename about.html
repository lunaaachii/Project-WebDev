About UniStay Manila

Helping students find affordable and comfortable apartments near Manila's University Belt.

Who We Are

UniStay Manila is an apartment finder specially created for students who are studying around the University belt in Manila. We realize that the task of locating the safe, affordable, and convenient place to reside might be hard, which is why we developed a platform that allows you to access apartment listings through one place.

Whether you are in search of a dormitory, condominiums, boarding house, or a studio apartment, UniStay can compare the different rental options based on location, price, and amenities.

Our Mission

To facilitate easier, faster and more reliable apartment searching for all students around Manila's University Belt.

Our Vision

To be the #1 apartment search website in Metro Manila by offering trusted apartment listings and great service to students.
